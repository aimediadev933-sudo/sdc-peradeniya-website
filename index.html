About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and commitment to excellence in staff development

History & Background

The Staff Development Center of the University of Peradeniya was established in March 1999 to bring about better collaboration among faculties with regard to staff development. The SDC serves as an umbrella organization for the different units of individual faculties.

🎯 Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in training for the development of efficient and effective personnel who could contribute significantly to their institution's growth and development.

🚀 Our Mission

To conduct and nurture activities that would promote and contribute to capacity building of members in a work force.

Our Objectives

  • Enhance teaching and learning methodologies in higher education
  • Develop leadership and management skills among academic staff
  • Provide continuous professional development opportunities
  • Foster collaboration between universities and institutions
  • Promote research and innovation in staff development

Our Programs

Comprehensive training programs designed to enhance skills and promote professional growth

CTHE

Certificate in Teaching in Higher Education

Comprehensive program designed for probationary academic staff to develop essential teaching skills and methodologies.

  • Teaching methodology and pedagogical techniques
  • Curriculum development and course design
  • Assessment and evaluation methods
  • Educational technology integration
  • Student engagement strategies
CCLMU

Certificate Course in Leading Managing Universities

Advanced leadership program for senior academic staff focusing on university management and strategic leadership.

  • Leadership skills and team management
  • Strategic planning and vision development
  • Change management and innovation
  • Decision making and problem solving
  • Financial management and resource allocation
SSC

Senior Student Counsellor Program

Specialized program for developing counseling skills and providing comprehensive student support services.

  • Counseling techniques and methodologies
  • Student psychology and development
  • Crisis intervention and management
  • Support system development
  • Mental health awareness
CBPTO

Capacity Building Program for Technical Officers

Technical skill enhancement program for laboratory and technical staff members across all disciplines.

  • Technical expertise development
  • Safety protocols and procedures
  • Equipment management and maintenance
  • Quality assurance and control
  • Innovation and problem solving
CBPMA

Capacity Building Program for Management Assistants

Administrative excellence program for management and support staff to enhance organizational efficiency.

  • Administrative skills and procedures
  • Project management techniques
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Digital literacy and technology
  • Customer service excellence
ENACT

ENACT Program

Comprehensive staff development program focusing on enhancing capabilities across all organizational levels.

  • Professional development planning
  • Skill enhancement workshops
  • Career advancement strategies
  • Performance improvement techniques
  • Leadership development pathway
