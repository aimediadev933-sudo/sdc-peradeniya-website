Director's Message

Prof. Roshan Ragel

Director, Staff Development Center

The Staff Development Center of the University of Peradeniya was established in March 1999 to bring about better collaboration among faculties with regard to staff development. As an umbrella organization for different units of individual faculties, the SDC conducts activities to promote and nurture staff development at the University of Peradeniya and other universities in the system.

Our commitment extends beyond traditional boundaries, and we now have the capability to offer our services for staff development in other institutes of the country as well.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in training for the development of efficient and effective personnel who could contribute significantly to their Institution's growth and development.

Our Mission

To conduct and nurture activities that would promote and contribute to capacity building of members in a workforce, ensuring continuous professional development and institutional excellence.
